Copper Tea Pot

#Tea #Drinker

Copper tea kettles are known for their elegant appearance. Besides, copper tea kettles help you boil water quicker than a stainless steel or glass kettle. That’s because copper is an excellent conductor of heat. It only needs medium heat to boil water, saving both time and energy.
